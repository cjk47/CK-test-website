Content Strategy

The bar for content keeps rising. Ranking today means fully answering the query, addressing real customer pain points, and bringing genuine insight, not just publishing more pages.

I start with the questions your audience is actually asking, audit your site for gaps, and build an end-to-end content plan designed to drive leads and revenue. From there, I help you build a process your team can actually run, so content gets done consistently and at scale.

  • Keyword research & opportunity mapping
  • Topic clusters & pillar page planning
  • Content briefs your writers can follow
  • Existing content audit & refresh recommendations

Technical SEO

How a site performs in search usually comes down to what's underneath it. Weak technical foundations quietly limit everything else, and the issues often go unnoticed until traffic drops or growth stalls.

My work covers crawl behavior, site speed, indexation, and how clearly search engines can read your site. I work with your engineering team to make sure issues get fixed, not just flagged.

  • Full site technical audit
  • Core Web Vitals & page speed
  • Structured data & schema markup
  • Indexability, crawl budget & canonicalisation
New & Emerging

AI Search Strategy

AI is reshaping how people discover information, compare options, and decide who to trust. ChatGPT, Claude, and AI Overviews are changing where answers come from, and most brands aren't visible in any of them.

The fundamentals haven't changed — both traditional search and generative AI reward the same thing: clarity and authority. That's where I focus, helping businesses stay visible as traffic naturally shifts toward LLM-driven journeys.

  • LLM visibility audit across ChatGPT, Claude & AI Overviews
  • Authority & E-E-A-T signal building
  • Content structured to be cited & recommended by AI
  • Competitor analysis across AI search platforms

Off-site SEO

On-page optimization can only take you so far. Long-term rankings require authority — and authority is built off your site, through links, mentions, and digital PR.

I build ethical, sustainable link profiles through genuine relationships, expert positioning, and content that's worth linking to.

  • Backlink profile audit & cleanup
  • Digital PR & link acquisition strategy
  • Competitor link gap analysis
  • Brand mention & unlinked citation recovery
