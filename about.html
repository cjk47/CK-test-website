How I work

My approach to SEO.

Not everything in SEO is worth doing. I focus on the work that has a real impact on your business, not just checking boxes or chasing minor improvements.

Visibility follows good experiences. I think about how real people use a site, not just how search engines crawl it — visitors should find what they're looking for quickly and leave with a good impression.
