About me

Hi, I'm Craig.

I'm a freelance SEO strategist based in Brooklyn, NY. I help founders, small teams, and growing businesses earn more organic visibility — without shortcuts, fluff, or cookie-cutter strategies.

I've been working in organic search for over thirteen years, across SaaS, e-commerce, media, professional services, and local businesses. I've seen what works, what doesn't, and how fast things change.

By day, I lead SEO at Bounteous, managing organic strategy for enterprise clients including Best Western, Chase, KKR, and Gerber. On the side, I take on a select number of freelance clients — bringing that same enterprise-level thinking to smaller teams who want it done properly.

What I care about most is building something sustainable — not a quick traffic spike that disappears with an algorithm shift, but a genuine, lasting presence that compounds over time.
